About NY Parliamentary Debate

Founded by Horace Mann, Dalton, and Stuyvesant in 2016, the New York Parliamentary Debate League (NYPDL) has grown to serve high school students from schools across the tristate area. The league organizes monthly, day-long tournaments in the parliamentary style.

About Parliamentary Debate

Parliamentary debate involves only twenty minutes of preparation and no outside sources, making it an event that emphasizes rhetoric, logic, and knowledge of current events. Parli is the most widespread debate event at the collegiate level and internationally.
